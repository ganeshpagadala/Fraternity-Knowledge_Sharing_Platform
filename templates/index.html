Chapter 01

Platform Overview & Philosophy

Fraternity is not a social network. It is a professional commons — a space where serious, verified professionals come with intention, not habit.

The Problem It Solves
📢

Noise & Clutter

Existing platforms are saturated with ads, sponsored content, and irrelevant posts that drown out genuine professional exchange.

🎭

Performative Behaviour

People optimise for visibility and applause rather than knowledge sharing, making discourse shallow and self-promotional.

🌐

Open to Everyone

No verification means low signal-to-noise ratio. Unqualified voices drown out expert ones. Trust is impossible to establish.

🤖

Algorithmic Manipulation

Virality is rewarded over substance. Platforms surface what gets clicks, not what is accurate, useful, or professionally relevant.

Core Philosophy
Professional-FirstEvery design decision prioritises professional value over engagement metrics, time-on-site, or virality.
Verified EntryAccess is earned by being a certified professional. The platform is not a public square — it is a members' room.
Subscription ModelThe subscription is not just revenue. It is a commitment filter. It removes casual, bad-faith, and low-intent participants.
No Ads — EverThere will never be ads, sponsored content, promoted posts, or any form of paid visibility. Revenue is entirely user subscriptions.
Author-Controlled ReachContent distribution is controlled by the author, not an algorithm. Reach is earned through genuine attention.
Intentional DesignEvery feature must serve professional purpose. No dark patterns, no addictive loops, no infinite scroll tricks.
Target Users

Fraternity is exclusively for professionals certified by statutory institutes via formal examinations.

Chapter 02

Access, Membership & KYC

Entry into Fraternity is not open. It is earned. The verification process ensures every member is who they say they are.

Registration Flow
Step 1→ Register with email or phone number
Step 2→ OTP verification
Step 3→ Select profession and certification type
Step 4→ Upload professional certificate + government ID
Step 5→ KYC review (automated OCR → manual review)
Step 6→ On approval → profile creation
Step 7→ Subscription payment (₹9/month or ₹99/year)
Step 8→ Full access granted
KYC Requirements
MandatoryKYC is non-negotiable. No verified identity = no access. No exceptions.
Documents RequiredProfessional certificate + valid government-issued ID (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport).
Verification MethodPhase 1: Manual admin review. Phase 2+: DigiLocker API / Aadhaar eKYC for automated verification.
Review TimeTarget: within 24 hours of submission.
Data PrivacyKYC documents stored encrypted. Never displayed to other users. Used only for verification.
Non-Negotiable

There is no free tier, no guest access, no trial without verification. The gated nature is a core feature — not a barrier to growth but a guarantee of quality.

Chapter 05

Home Feed & Content Discovery

The feed is the heart of Fraternity. Chronological, domain-focused, and free of algorithmic manipulation.

Feed Toggle — Same Domain vs Inter Domain
Same DomainDefault state. Shows posts only from peers in the user's own profession. Clean, focused, directly relevant.
Inter DomainOpt-in toggle. Shows posts from professionals outside the user's own domain. Enables cross-professional discovery.
FollowingChronological feed from people you explicitly follow, across all domains.
No Algorithmic RankingStrictly chronological. No engagement-based ordering. Ever.
Chapter 06

Post Types

Fraternity supports distinct post formats, each designed for a specific kind of professional exchange.

Standard Post
Content TypesText (rich or plain), images (up to 4), videos (up to 4), documents.
@MentionsTag peers directly in posts using @username — they receive a notification and a link to the post.
ReactionsLike, repost, quote, comment, bookmark — all actions tracked privately.
ViewsPost view counts are tracked automatically when a peer opens the thread.
Quote PostQuote another peer's post with your own commentary attached above the original.
DeleteAuthors can delete any of their own posts at any time. No recovery.
Chapter 07

The Boom Mechanism

Boom is Fraternity's answer to the algorithm. Author-controlled, deliberate, without engagement manipulation.

⚡ Instant Boom

Post is immediately pushed to the home feeds of all relevant peers in the author's domain.

Author posts → ALL domain peers receive it in feed immediately. No delay. Simultaneous reach.

🌊 Ripple Boom

Post propagates gradually. A new peer receives the post only after the previous peer has viewed it.

Author defines group [A,B,C,D,E] → A receives → A views → B receives → B views → C… Reach grows through real attention.

Chapter 10

Interface & Navigation Structure

Designed to feel intentional — like a tool you come to with purpose and leave when your work is done.

Design Principles
  • Clarity over decoration — UI exists to serve function, not to impress
  • Intent-driven interaction — every tap should have clear purpose and outcome
  • No infinite scroll tricks — clear end-of-feed state, no forced content recycling
  • Clean, professional aesthetic — not playful or gamified. Feels like a serious professional tool
Chapter 11

Governance & Moderation

Verified professionals, who have paid to be here, will largely govern themselves.

The Three-Strike System
Any Peer→ Flags a post or behaviour as unprofessional
Admin→ Reviews flagged content
Strike 1→ Formal warning issued. Notified in-app and email.
Strike 2→ Second formal warning. Informed of consequence of next strike.
Strike 3→ Account removed. Access revoked. Subscription cancelled.
Chapter 12

What Fraternity Is Not

These boundaries are permanent design decisions.

🚫

Not a Mass-Market Platform

Scale is not the goal. 10,000 engaged verified professionals is worth more than 10 million passive anonymous accounts.

🚫

Not Ad-Driven

No advertisements. No sponsored content. No promoted posts. Ever. Revenue comes only from subscriptions.

🚫

Not Algorithmically Addictive

No infinite scroll, no dopamine-loop notifications, no content ranking designed to maximise time-on-site.

🚫

Not Based on Popularity Metrics

No follower counts displayed, no leaderboards, no trending, no "most liked" sections.

🚫

Not Open to Everyone

The closed, verified nature is a permanent feature — not a phase to be outgrown as the platform scales.

🚫

Not a Job Board

Fraternity is for professional exchange and knowledge — not for job listings or commercial transactions.
